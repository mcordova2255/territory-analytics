Rolls-Royce plc is the principal contract-signing entity for civil aerospace, defence and submarines. Power Systems (mtu brand) operates semi-independently with its own UK entity (Rolls-Royce Solutions UK Limited).

## F) PROCUREMENT / PURCHASING CONTROL

Rolls-Royce operates a **hybrid procurement model** — group-level strategic frameworks with divisional execution. The 2018 restructuring explicitly created "three simpler decentralised units" (civil aerospace, defence, power systems) with rationalised back-office functions, indicating a move away from fully centralised procurement toward divisional autonomy under group standards.

**Procurement is best described as: Hybrid (central standards, divisional execution).** Group policies govern supplier compliance, ethics and major strategic sourcing. Day-to-day contract execution sits within each division.

**Key UK entities likely to sign major contracts:** Rolls-Royce plc (CH 01003142) is the principal trading company and the most likely signatory for civil aerospace, defence and submarine contracts in the UK. Rolls-Royce Solutions UK Limited (CH 04691779) likely signs contracts for the Power Systems/mtu product range. A single group-wide procurement owner entity is not explicitly named in accessible sources beyond Rolls-Royce plc as the principal operating company.
