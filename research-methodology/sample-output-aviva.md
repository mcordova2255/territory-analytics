## B) PARENTS & CONTROL

The group's operating entities — including Aviva Insurance Limited (SC002116), Aviva Life & Pensions UK Limited (03253947), and the Irish DACs — are all subsidiaries of Aviva plc as confirmed in the Annual Report 2024 subsidiary notes and each entity's CH PSC filings showing Aviva plc or an intermediate holding company as PSC.

**Controlling shareholders:** Aviva plc is a widely-held FTSE 100 listed company with no disclosed controlling shareholder in accessible CH filings. Institutional shareholders are not individually named in CH PSC records for a listed company of this type. No family, PE or individual holds a majority stake. This is portfolio shareholding only — not operational control.

## F) PROCUREMENT / PURCHASING CONTROL

Aviva operates a **hybrid procurement model**. The group has a named central services entity — Aviva Central Services UK Limited (CH 03259447, SIC 96090, Norwich) — which handles group-wide shared services including IT infrastructure, facilities management and procurement functions. This entity was formerly Norwich Union Central Services Limited, indicating it has held the group services role since pre-rebrand. The equivalent in Ireland is Aviva Group Services Ireland Limited (CRO 322579, Cherrywood), which handles Irish shared services.

**Procurement is best described as: Hybrid (centralised standards and shared services, divisional operational execution).** Technology and facilities procurement is likely centralised through the Central Services entities. Product-line procurement (insurance claims supply chain, investment management) sits within the relevant operating divisions.

**Key UK & IE entities likely to sign major contracts:** Aviva Central Services UK Limited (CH 03259447) for group-wide technology, facilities and supplier contracts in the UK. Aviva Insurance Limited (SC002116) for general insurance claims supply chain contracts. Aviva Life & Pensions UK Limited (03253947) for pension and life product supplier contracts. In Ireland, Aviva Group Services Ireland Limited (CRO 322579) for shared services contracts; Aviva Insurance Ireland DAC (CRO 605769) for Irish general insurance supply chain.
